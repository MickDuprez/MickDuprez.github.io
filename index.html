MetaCAD

Introducing MetaCAD, the revolutionary new CAD application that is currently under development and set to take 3D modeling, BIM, and user customization to the next level.

With MetaCAD, you can expect to easily create complex 3D models with intuitive tools and a user-friendly interface. The application's powerful BIM capabilities will allow you to design and manage buildings and infrastructure with precision and efficiency.

But what really sets MetaCAD apart is its extensive customization options. Using JSON and JavaScript/TypeScript, you will be able to tailor the application to your specific needs and workflow, making it the perfect fit for any design professional. And with cloud-based model data storage powered by MongoDB, you'll be able to access your projects from anywhere, on any device.

Whether you're an architect, engineer, or designer, MetaCAD will have everything you need to bring your vision to life. Keep an eye out for its release and be one of the first to try it out and see the difference for yourself.
